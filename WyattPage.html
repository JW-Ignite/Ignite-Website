Forza Horizon 5

Forza Horizon 5 is an open-world racing game developed by Playground Games and released in November 2021. Set in a vibrant recreation of Mexico, it features the largest and most varied map in the Horizon series, with deserts, jungles, beaches, ancient ruins, and even a volcano. Players can choose from hundreds of cars and take part in a wide range of activities, from road races and off-road adventures to drifting challenges or casual exploration. The dynamic seasons and weather bring the world to life, making each drive feel fresh and unpredictable. The game also emphasizes creativity and community. Players can advance through the Horizon Adventure campaign, join online races, or try unique challenges created by others through the EventLab system. With its striking visuals, diverse car roster, and seamless multiplayer features, Forza Horizon 5 delivers both structured racing and open-ended freedom, appealing to casual drivers and dedicated fans alike.
