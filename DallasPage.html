RimWorld

General Description

Rimworld is a top-down survival and colony builder game. It's set roughly 3,000 years into the future on a lawless planet at the edge of explored space. The player controls colonists who, in a traditional playthrough, crashland after their ship is destroyed. The player must mae sure they survive and either thrive or escape the chaotic world.

DLCs

Rimworld has several DLCs which expand on both lore and gameplay.
